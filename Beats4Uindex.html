All Out of Love

Air Supply, Album Released: June 1980
Reviewed by Anne Cao

All out of love, the one hit wonder that has become the go to healing song for people in heartbreak all over the world. Penned by the iconic Air Supply, this song is an ode to a lost love with a memorable chorus that will leave you humming it non-stop. With a simple acoustic guitar and piano backing, it highlights the vocals of the lead singers, and the orchestral arrangement at the climax concludes the song as an epic and leaves you wanting to loop it again.
